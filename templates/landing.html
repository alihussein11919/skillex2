Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Skillex and how our platform works.

How does skill exchange work?
Skill exchange on Skillex allows users to connect and trade knowledge directly. Simply find a partner, agree on terms, and start learning!
Is skill exchange completely free?
Yes, basic skill exchanges are free. However, some expert lessons or resources in the marketplace may have a fee.
How do I know if a teacher is qualified?
Each teacher has a profile with reviews, ratings, and verified credentials where applicable. Always check profiles before starting.
Can I exchange skills remotely?
Absolutely! Skillex supports both in-person and remote exchanges through video calls and messaging.
What if I'm not satisfied with an exchange?
If you're not satisfied, you can leave a review and report any issues. Our team will assist in resolving disputes when necessary.
What skills can I learn or teach on Skillex?
You can exchange a wide range of skills including languages, arts, crafts, professional skills, and more. Explore categories to see what's available.
