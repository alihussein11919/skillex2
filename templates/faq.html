Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Skillex

What is Skillex?

Skillex is an online platform that connects skilled teachers with eager learners. We facilitate one-on-one lessons, group classes, and educational resource sharing across various subjects and skills.

How do I get started with Skillex?

Getting started is easy! Simply create an account, choose whether you want to teach or learn, complete your profile, and start exploring our marketplace of courses and teachers.

What subjects are available on Skillex?

We offer a wide range of subjects including languages, programming, music, arts, academic subjects, professional skills, and more. Our marketplace is constantly growing with new subjects and skills.

How do I become a teacher on Skillex?

To become a teacher, create a teacher account, complete your profile with qualifications and experience, create your course offerings, and submit them for approval. Once approved, you can start accepting students.

How much can I earn as a teacher?

Teachers set their own rates and keep 80% of their earnings. Your income potential depends on your expertise, teaching hours, and rates. Many of our teachers earn a substantial income through both live sessions and digital resources.

What tools do I need to teach online?

You'll need a reliable internet connection, a computer with a webcam and microphone, and any specific tools related to your subject (e.g., digital drawing tablet for art classes). We provide the virtual classroom platform.

How do I find the right teacher?

You can browse teachers by subject, read reviews, filter by price and availability, and even book trial lessons. Our search filters help you find teachers that match your learning style and goals.

Can I schedule lessons at my convenience?

Yes! You can view teacher availability in your time zone and book lessons that fit your schedule. Many teachers offer flexible scheduling options, including evenings and weekends.

What happens if I need to cancel a lesson?

Lessons can be cancelled or rescheduled up to 24 hours before the scheduled time for a full refund. Late cancellations may be subject to our cancellation policy.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. All payments are processed securely through our platform to protect both teachers and students.

How does the payment process work?

Payment is collected when you book a lesson. Funds are held securely and released to the teacher 24 hours after the lesson is completed successfully.

Are there any additional fees?

We charge a small platform fee to cover payment processing and platform maintenance. All fees are clearly displayed before you make a payment.

What are the technical requirements?

You need a stable internet connection (minimum 5Mbps), a modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari), and a device with a camera and microphone for video lessons.

How do I join a virtual classroom?

Simply click the lesson link in your dashboard or email reminder 5 minutes before the scheduled time. Our virtual classroom opens in your browser - no downloads required.

What if I experience technical issues?

Our support team is available 24/7 to help with technical issues. You can also check our troubleshooting guide or contact us through live chat for immediate assistance.
